Submitting Apps to Decent Portal

Update - content out of date.

Three Paths to the Portal

We created the Decent Launch Program to get your app onto the Decent Portal. But maybe it doesn't fit your needs. For example:

  • You'd like to develop at your own pace and a 90-day program duration doesn't suit you. 
  • The guidance and promotion aspects of the program are less valuable to you.

If you like the Decent style of app, you can always use our open source create-decent-app tool to get a project started quickly. It's free open source that can be used by anyone for any purpose. 

You might also want to have your app on our portal, enjoying free hosting, deployment tools, and user discovery. You can accomplish this without joining the Decent Launch Program. Your app just needs to meet our requirements. When your app is ready, you can submit it to us for review. And we're happy to answer questions along the way.

One other potential tweak you might like - work on your app for a while, and then join the Decent Launch Program when you feel you are within 90 days of releasing.

App Requirements for Decent Portal

Accepted apps can be nearly anything—tools, games, art, or technical experiments.

They must meet some requirements that allow us to launch them on our Decent Portal in keeping with the Safe+Easy+Playful vision. The main requirement that might be surprise some developers is that apps should not call services.

See Decent Portal App Requirements for the complete list and some explanation.

Submitting Your App

Email us at launch@decentapps.net to start a conversation about submitting your app.

But also you don't need to wait for your app to be ready before talking to us. Send any questions, tell us about your app, or simply introduce yourself whenever you like.
